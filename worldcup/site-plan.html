Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to generate awareness for the 2026 FIFA World Cup through sharing information about where the event is, why it is being held, and what it is.

Audience

Our audience is anyone who has any interest in the event. This includes sports fans, football fans, tourists, locals to the host nations of The United States, Mexico, and Canada. The audience will be people who want to learn more about the event and where it is being held. My audience will visit my website primarily on desktop as the purpose is planning and information.

Home page

The 2026 Fifa World Cup. Hosted in The United States, Mexico, and Canada. The 2026 World Cup takes place June 11th-July 19th. This will be the first World Cup to host 48 teams! The new expanded format opens the cup to more nations than ever before. Here, you will find out more about the upcoming 2026 World celebration of Football. We will provide information regarding host sites, cities, and a brief explenation of what the tournament is and why it is played. The 2026 Iteration of the Fifa World Cup will feature 48 teams from the 6 regions of the world who will be selected through the FIFA approved qualification process. These teams who qualify will be split into 12 groups of 4. These teams will play each other to earn a spot in the top 32. These top 32 teams will be placed into a single elimination bracket based on performance and clash to advance to the end. The defending champions, Argentina, will look to retain their title while 47 other nations will look to knock them off their pedistoll and claim glory for their respective countries. The World Cup is seem and viewed as the defacto World Championship of Football and crowns the top Country in the World of Football.

What is The World Cup?

What is the World Cup? The World Cup is a celebration of the best football has to offer. The World Cup tournament originated in the year 1930. Uruguay took home the inagural trophy. The tournament has had many winners, but the country with the most is Brazil with 5 titles. The World Cup is a global Football tournament held every 4 years. Each region sends a certain amount of qualifying countries where teams represent their countries in the biggest sporting event on the planet. Players get the chance to return to their home countries, put a team together, and represent their homes on the world stage in a tournament that pins up each country against the other for the chance to be crowned World Cup Champion. This tournament provides a chance for the top Footballers in the world to take a break from their club team and play for their home nation. The teams are split into groups of 4 and play to advance to the 32 team knockout stage. From there, its win or go home until one team remains. It is one of the toughest and unforgiving tournament formats in all of sports that ensures each team must be at the top of their game every day. The tournament is a global celebration that brings us all together in celebration of the beautiful game that unites the beautiful world.
