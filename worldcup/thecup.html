What is The World Cup?

What is the World Cup? The World Cup is a celebration of the best football has to offer. The World Cup tournament originated in the year 1930. Uruguay took home the inagural trophy. The tournament has had many winners, but the country with the most is Brazil with 5 titles. The World Cup is a global Football tournament held every 4 years. Each region sends a certain amount of qualifying countries where teams represent their countries in the biggest sporting event on the planet. Players get the chance to return to their home countries, put a team together, and represent their homes on the world stage in a tournament that pins up each country against the other for the chance to be crowned World Cup Champion. This tournament provides a chance for the top Footballers in the world to take a break from their club team and play for their home nation. The teams are split into groups of 4 and play to advance to the 32 team knockout stage. From there, its win or go home until one team remains. It is one of the toughest and unforgiving tournament formats in all of sports that ensures each team must be at the top of their game every day. The tournament is a global celebration that brings us all together in celebration of the beautiful game that unites the beautiful world.
