2026 World Cup

The 2026 Fifa World Cup. Hosted in The United States, Mexico, and Canada. The 2026 World Cup takes place June 11th-July 19th. This will be the first World Cup to host 48 teams! The new expanded format opens the cup to more nations than ever before. Here, you will find out more about the upcoming 2026 World celebration of Football. We will provide information regarding host sites, cities, and a brief explenation of what the tournament is and why it is played. The 2026 Iteration of the Fifa World Cup will feature 48 teams from the 6 regions of the world who will be selected through the FIFA approved qualification process. These teams who qualify will be split into 12 groups of 4. These teams will play each other to earn a spot in the top 32. These top 32 teams will be placed into a single elimination bracket based on performance and clash to advance to the end. The defending champions, Argentina, will look to retain their title while 47 other nations will look to knock them off their pedistoll and claim glory for their respective countries. The World Cup is seem and viewed as the defacto World Championship of Football and crowns the top Country in the World of Football.
